Welcome to LOLDOKU
Rules
The goal of LOLDOKU is to fill in each of the 9 squares/boxes with a correct champion. You only have 3 Lives, and each time you guess incorrectly you will lose a life. The game ends when you fill in the grid or lose all lives.
What to Guess?
You must guess a Champion that matches both the row and column categories. For example, if column 1 is labeled 'TOP' and row 2 is labeled 'Demacia', then 'Garen' would be a valid answer for that intersection (box 4) because Garen is both a top lane champion and from Demacia.
Tooltips
If you are unsure what a particular category requires, consider hovering over the category Icon to reveal a Tooltip that should help explain the category in more detail.
Categories
Generally speaking there are five main categories: Lore, Kit, skin, Esports and Misc. Lore involves categories to do with a Champions Lore like region or species. Kit involves categories to do with champions abilities and functionalities. Skins involves categories that limit guesses to champions within a certain skinline ('e.g pool party only champions'). Esports categories revolve around champion data from esports events such as Worlds. Miscellaneous categories are any category that does not fit into these.
